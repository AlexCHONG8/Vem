1. Purpose

The purpose of this Design Verification and Validation Master Plan (V/VMP) is to document the required tests for a formal product related project. This plan lists which tests shall be conducted by SUMMED for design, process and user V/V, as required by the FDA Quality System Regulation (21 CFR 820) and ISO 13485:2016. This V/VMP is designed for the medical device constituent of a combination product.

The Verification and Validation activities are driven from input approved at Design Review:

  • The Design Input Requirement (DIR), document no. SM100-005-DIR-001.
  • The User Requirement Specification (URS), document no. 100-005-URS-001.
  • Product Requirement Specification document no. 100-005-PRS-001.
  • Risk Assessment for Management plan, and FMEA (DFMEA, PFMEA).

3. Definitions

  • Design Validation: Establishing by objective evidence that device specifications conform to user needs and intended use(s). Completed under actual or simulated use conditions.
  • Design Verification: Establishing by objective evidence confirming that the design output meets the design input requirements.
  • Process Validation: Demonstrating that a process when operated within specified limits results in product complying with requirements.
  • Tool/Equipment Validation: Checking that a tool or equipment meets specifications and fulfills its intended purpose.
