1. Purpose

To verify that Pharma A project product performance meets expected requirements during shelf life under specified storage conditions using accelerated aging methodology.

2. Scope

Applicable to Pharma A project product accelerated aging evaluation, including accelerated aging and product performance verification.

3. Proposed Validity Period

Shelf Life: 30 months (2.5 years)

4. Test Requirements

4.1 Environmental Conditions

Laboratory temperature and humidity must be controlled within 25 ± 3°C and 55 ± 20% RH range. All testing equipment must be within calibration validity period.

6. Accelerated Aging Test

According to Chapter 3 requirements, product shelf life is set to 2.5 years (30M). According to QW-QD-07 "Product Stability Validation Management Regulations" and material characteristics, Simject product accelerated aging temperature is determined as 55°C, humidity chamber controlled at 50% (±5%) RH.

Time Points: Accelerated 0 year, accelerated 1 year, accelerated 2 years, accelerated 2.5 years

Test Batches: 1 batch

Test Quantity: 30 units per batch

Product performance testing shall be conducted at accelerated aging time points of 0 days, 46 days, 91 days, and 114 days according to above requirements.

Test Parameter Simulated 0-Year Shelf Life (T0) Simulated 1-Year Shelf Life (T1) Simulated 2-Year Shelf Life (T2) Simulated 2.5-Year Shelf Life (T2.5)
Accelerated aging temperature 55 ± 2°C 55 ± 2°C 55 ± 2°C 55 ± 2°C
Accelerated aging time 0 days 46 days 91 days 114 days

8. Test Items and Test Methods

8.1 Auto-Injector Assembly

Assemble conditioned front and rear components with batch XX pre-filled syringe (PFS) according to "000-000-TIS-001 Simject Manual Final Assembly Fixture Test Instruction V1.0" to obtain finished auto-injectors.

8.2 Product Performance Testing

After accelerated condition treatment per above requirements, products shall undergo performance testing per following table:

No. Test Item Acceptance Criteria Test Method
1 Fill 1 ± 0.03 mL, single injection 0.27-0.34 mL 0.27 < injection volume < 0.34 mL 100-005-TIS-023 V1.0
2 Needle extension length (during injection): ≥0.5 in (12.70 mm) 0.6 in (±0.1 in) / 15.24 mm (±2.54 mm)
3 Cap removal force: 5-35 N 5-35 N
4 Injection activation force (pressing needle shield): 5-25 N 5-25 N
5 Injection time for 0.3 mL dose: ≤2 seconds ≤2 seconds
6 Drug visibility before use for inspection Viewing window clearly identifiable
7 Post-injection needle shield lockout force >2× injection activation force >50 N
8 User ability to distinguish device status (unused/in use/used/disabled) After injection, PFS stopper visible at window
9 Needle shielding When contacting test surface, needle not visually visible; when not contacting, needle does not protrude
10 Injection completion indication (visual/auditory/tactile or combination) Injection start audible prompt, measured at 30 cm from device, loudness ≥60 dB
11 Sharps injury protection ISO 23908-2024 appendix test method

9. Re-validation Situation Description

When product raw materials change, production process flow changes, and shelf life may be affected, re-validation is required.
