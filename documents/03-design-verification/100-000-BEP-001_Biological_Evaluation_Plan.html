1. Purpose

To collect and analyze all applicable information on raw materials and processing techniques used in the Xinjieda Auto-Injector. Based on the target market where the product is intended to enter, conduct biological evaluation planning for the Auto-Injector in accordance with GB/T 16886.1-2022 or ISO 10993-1:2018 or EN ISO 10993-1:2020 to ensure product safety and effectiveness.

2. Scope

This document is applicable to the biological evaluation of the Xinjieda Auto-Injector.

3. Product Description

3.1 Product Name and Model

Product Name: Auto-Injector

3.2 Intended Use and Target Population

3.2.1 Intended Use

The Xinjieda Auto-Injector is suitable for use with Epinephrine (pre-filled 0.3 mL) for fluid delivery (intramuscular injection) for single patient or individual use only.

3.2.2 Target Population

Used for rescuing patients with emergency allergic reactions (such as anaphylactic shock, asthma), cardiac arrest, etc. (minors and adults). Contraindicated for patients with infection, inflammation, or broken skin at the injection site.

3.3 Product Composition and Structure

The Xinjieda Auto-Injector consists of front component, rear component, and cap component. The injection actuator in the rear component is composed of two parts: syringe carrier and power spring device.

Figure 3.3-1: Auto-Injector Product Structure

3.4 Packaging Information

Xinjieda Auto-Injector packaging includes support tray, carton, and outer shipping box.

3.5 Sterilization Information

The Xinjieda Auto-Injector is provided non-sterile and does not involve sterilization activities.

3.6 Evaluation of Shelf Life and Transportation Storage Effects on Biological Compatibility

Xinjieda Auto-Injector transportation and storage conditions:

1. Storage conditions: Temperature: 5°C ~ 40°C; Humidity: ≤ 80%

2. Transportation mode: Air or land transport

The biological compatibility of the Xinjieda Auto-Injector will not be affected by shelf life.

Justification: The polymer materials PC+ABS and PC used in the Xinjieda Auto-Injector product are not prone to degradation under normal use conditions. Based on their physical characteristics, the biological compatibility of polymer materials in the Xinjieda Auto-Injector will not be affected by shelf life.

5. Material Safety Research

5.1 PC

Polycarbonate (PC) is a high-performance thermoplastic with excellent physical and chemical properties, widely used in industrial, electronic, medical, construction, and other fields. It has high strength and strong impact resistance. Long-term service temperature can reach 120°C, short-term can withstand > 140°C, but is easily corroded by organic solvents (such as acetone, strong alkali). Long-term exposure to ultraviolet light causes yellowing. Its cytotoxicity, sensitization, and irritation test results (such as MTT method, intracutaneous reaction test) comply with ISO 10993-5, -10, -23 requirements. Generally has good biocompatibility, but attention should be paid to residual monomer bisphenol A (BPA) and potential hydrolytic degradation risks.

See Appendix A "PC Material Review Report"

5.2 PC+ABS

Polycarbonate-Acrylonitrile-Butadiene-Styrene Copolymer (PC+ABS) combines the strength and heat resistance of polycarbonate (PC) with the toughness of ABS, widely used in medical devices (such as housings, handles, respiratory equipment, etc.). Its cytotoxicity, sensitization, and irritation test results (such as MTT method, intracutaneous reaction test) comply with ISO 10993-5, -10, -23 requirements. Generally has good biocompatibility, but attention should be paid to the risk that residual monomers (acrylonitrile, bisphenol A) may affect results.

PC+ABS is widely used in short-term contact devices, suitable for non-implantable devices (such as equipment housings, handles, respiratory masks, etc.). PC+ABS has been successfully used in market-approved devices such as insulin pens and nebulizer housings. The expected biological response level is acceptable.

See Appendix B "PC+ABS Material Review Report"
