1. Safety Data Sheet (SDS Summary)

Trademark: CYCOLOY™ (C1200HF-111)
Description: Poly(bisphenol-A-carbonate) / Poly(acrylonitrile-butadiene-styrene) blend
Revision Date: 22-Jun-2023 | Manufacturer: SABIC Innovative Plastics

SECTION 1: Identification

Product Name: C1200HF-111
Recommended use: Molded or extruded articles. industrial products.
Company: SABIC Innovative Plastics US LLC, One Plastics Avenue, Pittsfield, MA 01201 USA.

SECTION 2: Hazards identification

Not considered hazardous by the 2012 OSHA Hazard Communication Standard (29 CFR 1910.1200). Hazardous components are bound in a thermoplastic resin matrix and are unlikely to contribute to workplace exposure.

SECTION 3: Composition/information on ingredients

  • Aluminum (CAS 7429-90-5): 1 - 5%
  • Carbon Black (CAS 1333-86-4): 0.3 - < 1.0%

SECTION 4: First aid measures

Inhalation: Move to fresh air. If symptoms persist, call a physician.
Skin contact: Immediately cool skin with cold water after contact with hot material. Wash with soap and water.
Eye contact: Flush with plenty of water for at least 15 minutes. If irritation persists, consult a specialist.
Ingestion: No hazards which require special first aid measures.

SECTION 5: Fire-fighting measures

Extinguishing media: Water spray mist or foam, dry chemical, CO2. Water is best for cooling.
Hazards from Combustion: Dense black smoke containing carbon oxides, hydrocarbon fragments, hydrogen cyanide, nitrogen oxides.

SECTION 6: Accidental release measures

Sweep up and shovel into suitable containers for disposal. Do not flush into surface water or sanitary sewer system.

SECTION 7: Handling and storage

Handling: Provide appropriate exhaust ventilation. Avoid dust formation. Earthing required for metal parts.
Storage: Store in closed container in a dry and cool area. Keep away from heat sources.

SECTION 8: Exposure controls/personal protection

Aluminum PEL: 5 mg/m³ (respirable), 15 mg/m³ (total). Carbon black PEL: 3.5 mg/m³. Wear protective gloves, safety glasses, and long-sleeved clothing.

SECTION 9: Physical and chemical properties

  • Physical State: Solid (Pellets)
  • Color: Varies
  • Odor: None or slight
  • Specific gravity: > 1; (water = 1)
  • Water solubility: Insoluble

SECTION 10: Stability and reactivity

Stable under ambient conditions. Hazardous polymerization does not occur. Avoid elevated temperatures. Process vapors may include trace hydrocarbons, phenols, styrene, and acrylonitrile.

SECTION 11: Toxicological information

LD50/oral/rat: >5000 mg/kg. LD50/dermal/rabbit: >2000 mg/kg. Carbon Black is classified as IARC 2B (possible human carcinogen), but bound in the plastic matrix.

SECTION 12: Ecological information

Ecological damages are not known or expected under normal use. Do not flush into surface water.

SECTION 13: Disposal considerations

Recycling is preferred. Landfill or incinerate in accordance with federal, state, and local requirements.

SECTION 14: Transport information

Not regulated as hazardous for shipment under current DOT, ADR, IMDG, or IATA guidelines.

SECTION 15: Regulatory information

Listed on TSCA (USA), DSL (Canada), EINECS (Europe), IECSC (China). Compliant with RoHS EU Directive 2011/65/EU.

SECTION 16: Other information

HMIS Rating: Health: 0, Flammability: 1, Reactivity: 0. Prepared by Product Stewardship & Toxicology.
