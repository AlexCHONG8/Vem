1. Product Safety Information (MSDS Summary)

Product Name: HYTREL® 6356 thermoplastic polyester elastomer
Reference: 150000004485 | Revision Date: 04.05.2024 | Version: 2.2

SECTION 1: Identification of the substance/mixture and of the company/undertaking

Use of the Substance/Mixture: Polymer for manufacturing and research use only.
Company: Performance Specialty Products Iberica S.L.U., Barcelona, Spain.

SECTION 2: Hazards identification

Not a classified substance or mixture according to Regulation (EC) No. 1272/2008. The product contains no PBT or vPvB components at levels of 0.1% or higher.

SECTION 3: Composition/information on ingredients

No dangerous components to be mentioned according to Regulations (EC) No. 1907/2006 and (EC) No. 453/2010. Pure polymer.

SECTION 4: First aid measures

Inhalation: Move to fresh air. If not breathing, give artificial respiration. Call a physician.
Skin contact: Cool skin rapidly with cold water after contact with molten material. Do not peel polymer from skin.
Eye contact: Immediately flush eyes with plenty of water for at least 15 minutes. Call a physician.
Ingestion: No specific intervention is indicated.

SECTION 5: Firefighting measures

Extinguishing media: Water, Foam, Dry chemical, Carbon dioxide (CO2).
Special hazards: Large molten masses may ignite spontaneously in air. Hazardous decomposition products include Carbon dioxide, Carbon monoxide, Acetic acid, Acrolein, and Propionaldehyde.

SECTION 6: Accidental release measures

Spilled material is a slipping hazard. Do not discharge to streams, ponds, lakes or sewers. Spills of fine material should be cleaned using gentle sweeping or vacuuming.

SECTION 7: Handling and storage

Handling: Open container only in well-ventilated area. Wash hands thoroughly after handling. Provide appropriate exhaust ventilation.
Storage: Store in a cool, dry place. Keep container closed to prevent contamination.

SECTION 8: Exposure controls/personal protection

General mechanical ventilation is normally adequate. Wear safety glasses with side shields. If there is a potential for contact with hot/molten material wear heat resistant clothing and footwear.

SECTION 9: Physical and chemical properties

  • Form: Pellets
  • Colour: Various
  • Odour: None
  • Melting point/range: >130°C
  • Thermal decomposition: >275°C
  • Water solubility: Insoluble

SECTION 10: Stability and reactivity

Stable at normal ambient temperature and pressure. Polymerization will not occur. Avoid temperatures >275°C.

SECTION 11: Toxicological information

The product contains no substances which at their given concentration, are considered to be hazardous to health.

SECTION 12: Ecological information

The product contains no substances classified as hazardous to the environment.

SECTION 13: Disposal considerations

Can be incinerated in compliance with local regulations. High fuel value makes incineration desirable.

SECTION 14: Transport information

Not classified as dangerous in the meaning of transport regulations (ADR, IATA, IMDG).

SECTION 15: Regulatory information

Not applicable to Seveso III directive. Water hazard class (Germany): nwg (not water endangering).

SECTION 16: Other information
