1. Product Safety Information (SDS Summary)

Trademark: LEXAN™ (HF1130-111)
Description: Poly (bisphenol-A-carbonate) [CASRN 111211-39-3]
Revision Date: 30-Aug-2011 | Manufacturer: SABIC Innovative Plastics

SECTION 1: Identification of the Substance/Mixture and of the Company

Product Name: LEXAN™ HF1130-111

Recommended Use: May be used to produce molded or extruded articles or as a component of other industrial products.

Supplier: SABIC Innovative Plastics

Address: One Plastics Avenue, Pittsfield, MA 01201 USA

Emergency Telephone: 800/447-4545

SECTION 2: Hazards Identification

Emergency Overview: Pellets with slight or no odor. Spilled material may create slipping hazard. Can burn in a fire creating dense, toxic smoke. Molten plastic can cause severe thermal burns. Fumes produced during melt processing may cause eye, skin, and respiratory tract irritation.

HMIS Rating: Health: 0, Flammability: 1, Reactivity: 0

SECTION 3: Composition/Information on Ingredients

Product Type: Mixture. Poly (bisphenol-A-carbonate) [CASRN 111211-39-3]. This product does not contain any ingredients requiring disclosure under national regulations.

SECTION 4: First Aid Measures

  • Inhalation: Move to fresh air in case of accidental inhalation of fumes from overheating or combustion. If symptoms persist, call a physician.
  • Skin Contact: Immediately cool the skin by rinsing with cold water after contact with hot material. Wash off immediately with soap and plenty of water. Consult a physician.
  • Eye Contact: Immediately flush with plenty of water for at least 15 minutes. If eye irritation persists, consult a specialist.
  • Ingestion: No hazards which require special first aid measures.

SECTION 5: Fire-Fighting Measures

Autoignition Temperature: 630°C (estimated)

Extinguishing Media: Water spray, Dry chemical, CO2. Water is the best extinguishing medium.

Special Hazards: Fire will produce dense black smoke containing hazardous combustion products: carbon oxides, hydrocarbon fragments.

SECTION 6: Accidental Release Measures

Methods for Cleaning Up: Sweep up and shovel into suitable containers for disposal. Do not create a powder cloud.

Environmental Precautions: Do not flush into surface water or sanitary sewer system.

SECTION 7: Handling and Storage

Handling: Handle in accordance with good industrial hygiene and safety practices. Provide for appropriate exhaust ventilation. Avoid dust formation.

Storage: Store in closed container in a dry and cool area. Keep away from heat sources and sources of ignition.

SECTION 8: Exposure Controls/Personal Protection

Engineering Measures: Handle in accordance with good industrial hygiene and safety practice. Provide for appropriate exhaust ventilation at machinery.

Personal Protective Equipment: Safety glasses, protective gloves, long sleeved clothing.

SECTION 9: Physical and Chemical Properties

  • Physical State: Solid (Pellets)
  • Appearance: Varies
  • Odor: None or slight
  • Specific gravity: > 1 (water = 1)
  • Water Solubility: Insoluble
  • Melting point: Softens gradually over a wide range of temperatures.

SECTION 10: Stability and Reactivity

Stability: Stable under ambient conditions. Hazardous polymerization does not occur.

Conditions to Avoid: Avoid temperatures above 320°C. Do not allow product to remain in barrel at elevated temperatures for extended periods of time.

Hazardous Decomposition Products: Process vapors may include trace levels of hydrocarbons, phenols, alkylphenols, diarylcarbonates.

SECTION 11: Toxicological Information

  • LD50/oral/rat: > 5000 mg/kg
  • LD50/dermal/rabbit: > 2000 mg/kg
  • Eye/Skin Contact: Not a hazard under normal industrial use. Pellets may be mechanically irritating to eyes.

SECTION 12: Ecological Information

Ecotoxicity: Ecological damages are not known or expected under normal use.

SECTION 13: Disposal Considerations

Waste Disposal: Recycling is encouraged. Landfill or incinerate in accordance with federal, state and local requirements.

SECTION 14: Transport Information

Transport Classification: Not regulated as hazardous for shipment under current transportation guidelines.

SECTION 15: Regulatory Information

International Inventories: TSCA (USA), DSL (Canada), EINECS (Europe), IECSC (China), etc. are all listed.

SECTION 16: Other Information

LEXAN™ is a trademark of SABIC Innovative Plastics.
