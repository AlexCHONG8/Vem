1. Product Safety Information (SDS Summary)

Product Name: DELRIN® FG500P NC010 Acetal Resin
Revision Date: 2018/11/26 | Version: 4.0
Manufacturer: DuPont China Holding Co., Ltd.

SECTION 1: Identification of the Substance/Mixture and of the Company

Product Name: DELRIN® FG500P NC010 Acetal Resin

Recommended Use: For household appliances, mechanical industry, food contact applications, and medical devices.

SECTION 2: Hazards Identification

GHS Classification: Not a hazardous substance or mixture according to GHS.

Other Hazards: Melting material may cause thermal burns. In case of overheating, hazardous fumes (formaldehyde) may be released.

SECTION 3: Composition/Information on Ingredients

Product Type: Mixture

Chemical Name CAS No. Concentration (%)
Polyoxymethylene (POM) 9002-81-7 > 99%
Formaldehyde 50-00-0 < 0.005%

SECTION 4: First Aid Measures

  • Inhalation: Move to fresh air in case of accidental inhalation of vapours. If symptoms persist, call a physician.
  • Skin Contact: If molten material contacts the skin, immediately flush with large amounts of cold water. Do not attempt to peel the material from the skin. Get medical attention.
  • Eye Contact: Flush eyes with water as a precaution. If eye irritation persists, consult a specialist.
  • Ingestion: No hazards which require special first aid measures.

SECTION 5: Fire-Fighting Measures

Extinguishing Media: Water spray, Dry chemical, Carbon dioxide (CO2).

Special Hazards: Hazardous decomposition products formed under fire conditions: Carbon oxides, Formaldehyde.

Protective Equipment for Firefighters: In the event of fire, wear self-contained breathing apparatus.

SECTION 6: Accidental Release Measures

Personal Precautions: Use personal protective equipment. Pellets on the floor may cause slipping.

Environmental Precautions: Avoid release to the environment.

Methods for Cleaning Up: Sweep up and shovel into suitable containers for disposal.

SECTION 7: Handling and Storage

Handling: Provide appropriate exhaust ventilation at machinery. Use personal protective equipment. Avoid overheating as it may release formaldehyde.

Storage: Keep in a dry, cool and well-ventilated place. Keep at temperatures far below 230°C.

SECTION 8: Exposure Controls/Personal Protection

Engineering Controls: Use localized exhaust ventilation to keep formaldehyde levels below exposure limits (0.3 ppm ceiling).

Personal Protective Equipment: Safety glasses, protective gloves when handling hot material.

SECTION 9: Physical and Chemical Properties

  • Appearance: Pellets (Natural color)
  • Odour: Slight, vinegar-like
  • Melting point: 175-183°C
  • Decomposition temperature: > 230°C
  • Water solubility: Insoluble
  • Density: 1.42 g/cm³

SECTION 10: Stability and Reactivity

Stability: Stable under normal conditions.

Conditions to Avoid: Temperatures above 230°C. Do not let molten material stay in the cylinder for a long time.

Incompatible Materials: Strong acids, bases, and oxidizing agents.

Hazardous Decomposition Products: Formaldehyde, Carbon monoxide, Carbon dioxide.

SECTION 11: Toxicological Information

Acute Toxicity: No data available. Oral (rat) LD50 > 11,000 mg/kg for POM resin.

Skin/Eye Irritation: Non-irritating under normal handling.

SECTION 12: Ecological Information

Ecotoxicity: Not expected to be harmful to aquatic organisms.

Persistence and Degradability: Not readily biodegradable.

SECTION 13: Disposal Considerations

Waste Treatment: Like most thermoplastic plastics, the product can be recycled. Can be disposed of as solid waste or incinerated according to local regulations.

SECTION 14: Transport Information

IMDG / IATA / ADR: Not classified as dangerous in the meaning of transport regulations.

SECTION 15: Regulatory Information

Complies with all known national regulations in China and major international chemical inventories.

SECTION 16: Other Information

Data provided is based on our current knowledge. Users are responsible for determining suitability for their specific application.
