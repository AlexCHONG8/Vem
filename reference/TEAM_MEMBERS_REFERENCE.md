## Document Approval Authority Matrix

### ISO13485 Document Classification

**Level 1 - Quality Manual**: General Manager approval
**Level 2 - Procedures**: Management Representative + Department Director approval
**Level 3 - Work Instructions**: Department Manager approval
**Level 4 - Records**: No approval required (controlled forms)

### Approval Authority by Role

**Level 1 Authority - General Manager (GM001)**:
- Quality Manual
- Quality Procedures (QP)
- Risk Management Plans (RMP)
- GSPR Checklist
- Instructions for Use (IFU)
- Major organizational changes

**Level 2 Authority - Department Directors (PD001, RD001)**:
- Validation Master Plans (VMP)
- Validation Plans (VP)
- Design Verification Plans (DVP)
- Product Lifecycle Validation Plans (PLVP)
- Department-level procedures

**Level 3 Authority - Management Representative (QM001)**:
- All quality system documents (review)
- Design Input Requirements (DIR)
- Biological Evaluation Plans (BEP)
- Risk management files
- Change control approvals

**Level 4 Authority - Department Managers (RD002, QA001)**:
- Standard Operating Procedures (SOP/SIP)
- Work instructions
- Test protocols (RTP, STP, FTP)
- Validation reports (VR)
- Test reports
